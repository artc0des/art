About Me

I enjoy the combination of creativity and organization that is required for a highly functional and visually appealing product to come to life. Over time, I’ve realized that I find joy in loosing track of time while turning ideas into organized and clean solutions through code. All the while, thinking about how the human that will be interacting with this thing is going to feel about it at the end of the day. I value growth - technical and otherwise. I’m big on visualization and creating systems to materialize the vision into reality. I value encouragement and belief, toward one-self and others.

I’m a senior software engineer with 9 years of experience primarily in fullstack development. My current work involves developing on-prem and cloud web applications for different clients using Go and Vue.js along with Docker containerization and high level system design. In the past I've had the opportunity to work on modernizing and maintaining user-centric logic tied to global navigation elements within schwab.com, a job that required consistent communication with over 7 different tech teams and knowledge in C# .NET Core as well as Typescript and React. I’ve also had the opportunity to implement CI/CD pipelines for multiple teams and write a few in-house tools with Java and Spring Boot.

I’m also investing time into learning about branding and business promotion through web design on my own. I'm discovering I enjoy the product design aspect of the prodcess along with the technical design side. Outside of technology, I’m implementing different habits that have taken a while to come into action, but I’m finally here. Meditation in the mornings, journaling on the weekends, rebuilding my relationship with money and being more patient with...me. Life. Change takes time.

When I have the extra time, I enjoy going to the coffee shop to re-group and take a look at what direction I’m taking with my life. I used to game quite a bit in my earlier years, now I play the occasional Diablo IV (currently thinking about getting the steam deck, but not fully sold yet!)

I also very much enjoy music - live and otherwise. What started off as a dance hobby evolved into a DJ hobby and eventually into a passion for music production. Feel free to check out my work!
